How to Play:
1. Choose a team and an actor.
2. Actor sees the prompt and acts it out silently (1 minute timer).
3. Teammates guess! If correct, click your team's button.
4. Play rounds until all prompts are used. Highest score wins!
Prompts are from the Nativity story (Luke 2 & Matthew 2).
